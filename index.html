HAG is a new queer live act from Bristol. A dramatic, experimental electro-gregorian, monk-punk, art-pop-EBM band known for hypnotic live shows that feel part séance part rave. HAG beam out powerful vocals, polyphonic harmonies, bass and synth, connecting wytches from the past present and future. Enter into our shroud of sound, with songs about aging, beauty, breasts, queerness, nature and the unity found in being the 'other'.

Made up of Viki Browne (performance artist/ director), S-J Burton (singer/ songwriter) & Cardboard (multi-instrumentalist/ painter).
